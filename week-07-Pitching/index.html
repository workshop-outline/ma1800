Week 7 — Project Pitching

This week is dedicated to pitching your web art project ideas. Each student will present their concept to the group, explaining their vision, process, and intended outcomes. The goal is to practise clear communication, get constructive feedback, and refine your ideas before moving into the making stage.

Key Points for Presenting

  • Clarity: Explain your idea in simple terms before going into detail.
  • Context: Connect your project to themes, references, or inspirations.
  • Process: Share how you plan to make it, including tools and techniques.
  • Visuals: Use sketches, mockups, or examples to illustrate your ideas.
  • Timing: Keep your presentation concise (5–7 minutes).
  • Engagement: Leave space for questions and feedback.

Remember, pitching is not about having a fully finished project — it’s about showing your thinking, testing ideas, and inviting dialogue.
