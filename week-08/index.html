Week 8 — Wireframing and Navigation

This week focuses on wireframing and navigation design. Wireframing is a way of sketching or planning how your website will be structured before you begin coding. It helps you organise content, test navigation, and refine user experience.

Building on the ideas you pitched in Week 7, these workshops will introduce techniques for advanced layout and content control, enabling you to bring your concepts closer to fully working prototypes. You will learn how to structure multiple pages, create navigation menus, and manage more complex design elements.
